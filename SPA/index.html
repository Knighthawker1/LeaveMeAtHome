Tom P. - Your Pet Walker

Leave Me at Home provides pet sitting, pet walking, and house sitting services in Bellevue, TN

Hello, my name is Tom Parham and I would be interested to in taking care of your pet. I have been a pet owner nearly all my life and truly understand just how important our pets are to us. Choosing the right person to care for your pet is very important.

Leave Me at Home pet walking and pet setting is something I started because of the loss of my Weimaraner Lilly. After boarding Lilly at a kennel for an overnight stay, the following morning I received a call from the kennel advising me that she had passed away in the night. Lilly and I were inseparable, and I truly believe she passed away from the stress of not being home and being away from me.

I was devastated and because of this loss I started my business so no one else would have to experience this same tragedy.

My schedule is very flexible and I have several references that I can give you as to the type of care I provide for your pet. You will find that I treat your pets as if they were my own. In addition, one of my clients is a Metro Police Officer!

Kind regards,
Tom Parham

Leavemeathome.com Services

  • House Sitting - If you are headed out of town, my personalized service allows me to take care of your pet at your home. Your pet prefers to stay in their home in the environment they are accustomed to as it gives them the secure feeling they need.
  • Drop in visits - Need help when you have a tight schedule or can't make it home at lunch to let your pet out? Give me a call and I will work you into my schedule.
  • Dog walking - Daily walks are available and these walks normally last 15 to 20 minutes. Longer walk times are also available.
  • Backup sitters - I have back up sitters that will care for your pets if I am not available (e.g. if I am on vacation). As a result you will not have to make other arrangements during these periods.
  • Daily updates will be sent to you via text
  • Refferences available upon request
